This is the HOBBIES page.

I have several hobbies including music, video games, watching anime, and sports!


Music is extremely important to me. My late uncle was my original inspiration for music as he was a music teacher at Westerly High School and a hell of a musician. He could sing like a god and play piano like a prodigy. He also owned a baby grand piano which started my interest in playing the piano. Entering high school I always wanted to sing as I believed I could be good at it and decided to join select chorus which only pushed me to loving music infinitely more.


Video games are also important to me! Some may say that video games are bad for your health, which is true, and not true as video games have helped me through some pretty dark times. I enjoy playing Call of Duty with THE BOYS, but mostly love single-player RPG's as I can do what I want, when I want. My top 3 favorite games are Skyrim, Minecraft, and Assassin's Creed Valhalla!


Anime has taken a special place in my heart. I used to dislike anime due to the stereotypes about people who watch anime, but boy must I say those people were wrong. I am no anime connoisseur but have watch some anime with incredible stories such as, Naruto, My Hero Academia, and Attack on Titan! My favorite is Naruto with MHA in second. Anime helped cure my boredom through quarantine and also made form a love for all anime!

Finally, I am a huge sports fan! I started to learn to play hockey and ice skate around age 2-3 and played all the way up until my freshman year in high school. Later that year I also picked up Volleyball which is now my favorite sport and has some pretty awesome perks for tall people like me! I played in my first 3 years of college at Rivier University and hope to maybe play in clubs leagues once I am settled at NEIT!
