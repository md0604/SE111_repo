This is the SCHOOLS page.

Let's start from the beginning. I attended Elementary school at Blackrock Elementary in Coventry RI and eventually attended ASFMS middle school not too far from there. High school was a large step up from middle school. I attended Coventry High School from 2014 to 2018. After graduating I had attended my "dream" school which was Rivier University in Nashua, New Hampshire. I studied in Criminal Justice until my third year in 2021 when I changed majors to Cybersecurity! I am very glad I did so since I am now in love with anything related to computers and also believe Cybersecurity to be the future of protection! I decided that Rivier was not a good fit for me as the cybersecurity program was brand new the year I changed majors and figured a school close to home would be better as I could save money and just be able to see my family a lot more.I decided to attend New England Institute of Technology and cannot express my love for the school in words. I feel excited to learn especially since my major is really interesting!
