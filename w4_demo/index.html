ABOUT the movie How to Train Your Dragon

HTTYD is a thrilling, heartfelt journey about a young viking, taught to despise dragons by his father. The young viking winds up taking down his first dragon, or so he thought. He then realizes his father's beliefs may have been misguided all along. An epic journey ensues.
