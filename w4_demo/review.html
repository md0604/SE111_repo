REVIEW of the movie, How to Train Your Dragon

After watching HTTYD I fell in love with the trilogy. This first movie is an incredible start to Hiccups journey. As a matter of fact, we sent Dovahkiin on a similar adventure! I would highly reccommend this movie to families and anyone who enjoys animated fantasy type movies. I have watched it several times with Master Borri, Einarth, Wulfgar and Grand Master Paarthurnax. We came to a consenus and would rate this movie a 10/10. This series has topped our movie list. -Argenir
